Three Collectives :

Organisations with Education focus from remote geographies are forming a collaborative to amplify their work, support each other through shared resources and joint projects.

A collaborative of three organisation with a common geographical focus in rural Karnataka to work on the holistic development (citizenship and civic action, life skills, education and financial literacy) of women and children.

The collaborative relief efforts began in May 2021 with the objective to ensure that families in marginalised communities, particularly pregnant and lactating women & children in the 0-5 age group and people with disabilities avail of a nutritious wellness package by providing relief rations.

Ashish Shalini Shrivastava

Founder, Director, Shiksharth

In the world of community service, we are at a stage where collaborative and collective action are going to create contextually relevant sustainable solutions. And while everyone talks about it, there have been little efforts towards the same. Initiaitves like Sahayog Foundation are the torch bearers when it comes to bringing organisations and people together for shared vision. As a member of Education Collective in remote geographies, we have been truly blessed to have Sahyaog team as our partners. This partnership has enabled us get more clarity, understanding each others identities and moving towards strengthening the collective. While they are brilliant with facilitation, managing polarities, their main strength for us has been the ability of empathise with the diversity in perspective and strong ability to listen. It has been a great learning experience for all of us a partners of Sahyog Foundation and we look forward to creating strong models of collaborative action.
