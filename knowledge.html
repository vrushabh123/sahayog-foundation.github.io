Sahayog Foundation believes all stakeholders should view partnerships as a vital component of their strategy and organisation development. Therefore, it aims to equip nonprofit leaders with the tools to bridge the gap between intent to collaborate and initiate to partner with other non-profits organisations.

It conducts workshops and masterclasses to cultivate partnerships and ecosystem awareness.

From its varied experience, the foundation has created various events, content and tools such as masterclasses, workshops, guidebooks, surveys, roundtables and so on to provide the non-profit leaders with insights and a learning environment to develop their understanding of partnership readiness.

Collaborating In The Social Sector Survey

A first of its kind online survey of 160 non-profit leaders was undertaken between December 2020 and January2021 to understand the sector’s mindset towards collaboration and the practices in place for partnerships.
