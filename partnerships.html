For NGOs, we provide capability building support for partnership preparedness and facilitate partnerships to amplify and/or deepen impact.

We work with them for a period of ten months to plan how they can achieve their outcomes through partnerships and increase effectiveness and once ready, we link them to suitable partners to scale solutions to achieve greater impact.

While the process is tailored based on the needs of the non-profit, our support generally entails:

Leadership Preparedness

Organisation Culture

Organisation Strategy

Programme Preparedness

Building Partnership sustainability

Facilitation of partnership

Review of partnership
